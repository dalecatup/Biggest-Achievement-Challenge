What is CBT?

The CBT (Compulsory Basic Training) is a one-day course in the United Kingdom that ensures new riders understand road safety, bike control, and essential skills for riding a motorbike. Upon successful completion, the DL196 certificate is awarded and allows to ride on public roads for two years (with an L plate) on motorbikes up to 125cc.
